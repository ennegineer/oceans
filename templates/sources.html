The datasets in this project are from the following sources:

https://globalfishingwatch.org/    This source provided the data we used on fishing vessels. GFW combines publicly available tracking data with data acquired through ship monitorying systems operated by governments and made available through partnerships they've secured.
https://www.fao.org/about/en/    The Food and Agriculture Organization is a specialized agency of the UN, leading international efforts to achieve food security.
https://www.wri.org/    World Resources Institute is a global research organization focused around global challenges such as food, water, energy, climate and ocean.
